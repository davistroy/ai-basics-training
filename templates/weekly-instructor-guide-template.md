## Troubleshooting Guide

### Technical Issues During Session

| Issue | Immediate Action | Backup Plan |
|-------|------------------|-------------|
| Screen share fails | Restart share, switch to application share | Have participant share their screen |
| Demo breaks | Switch to screenshots | Walk through conceptually |
| Audio issues | Ask participants to type questions | Continue with video only |
| Slides won't advance | Use keyboard shortcuts | Open backup copy |

### Participant Challenges

| Challenge | Signs | Response |
|-----------|-------|----------|
| Confusion | Silence, confused looks, "I don't understand" | Pause, rephrase, use different example |
| Disengagement | Cameras off, no responses | Ask direct question, add interactive element |
| Falling behind | Mentions incomplete prerequisites | Offer 1:1 support, don't hold up group |
| Over-participation | One person dominating | "Let's hear from someone else" / "Great point, let's move on" |
| Technical difficulties | Can't access tools | Pair with another participant, follow up after |

### Content Challenges

| Challenge | Signs | Response |
|-----------|-------|----------|
| Content too easy | "We know this" | Speed up, add advanced examples |
| Content too hard | Many questions, confusion | Slow down, use simpler examples first |
| Off-topic tangent | Discussion drifting | "Great discussion - let's take it to [channel] and stay on track here" |
| Time pressure | Running behind | Skip [specified content], reference in participant guide |

# Appendix: Instructor Guide Template Guidelines

## Purpose of the Instructor Guide

The instructor guide enables facilitators to:

1. **Prepare effectively** - Know exactly what to do before, during, and after the session
2. **Stay on time** - Clear timing and flexibility guidance
3. **Handle questions** - Anticipated Q&A with prepared responses
4. **Troubleshoot issues** - Quick reference for common problems
5. **Track progress** - Tools for monitoring participant advancement
6. **Improve continuously** - Mechanisms for capturing and implementing feedback

## Content Guidelines

### Preparation Section
- Be specific about what needs testing
- Include realistic time estimates
- Provide checklists that can be completed sequentially

### Timing Section
- Always total to exactly 45 minutes
- Identify flex points explicitly
- Include recovery strategies for timing issues

### Delivery Section
- Write scripts as natural speech, not bullet points
- Include what to SAY, not just what to COVER
- Anticipate energy levels and attention spans

### Q&A Section
- Include actual questions from previous cohorts
- Keep answers concise (30-60 seconds each)
- Have deflection strategies for out-of-scope questions

### Troubleshooting Section
- Cover both technical and human challenges
- Provide immediate actions and backup plans
- Include observable signs to watch for

## Quality Checklist

Before using each weekly instructor guide:

- [ ] All timing adds up correctly
- [ ] Demo instructions are step-by-step
- [ ] Q&A covers likely questions
- [ ] Backup plans exist for technical failures
- [ ] Post-session tasks are actionable
- [ ] Links to related materials work
- [ ] Previous session feedback incorporated
- [ ] Version history updated
