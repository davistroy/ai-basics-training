## Key Concepts

### Concept 1: Block 1 Capstone Requirements

**Definition:**
The capstone demonstrates your mastery of AI prompting through a personal prompt library.

**Required Components:**

| Component | Requirement | Status Check |
|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| GitHub Repository | Organized, documented | ☐ |
| Prompt Templates | 5+ documented, tested | ☐ |
| `style.json` | Complete, tested | ☐ |
| Quality Rubrics | 2+ with specific criteria | ☐ |
| Task Priority Matrix | Analysis of opportunities | ☐ |

**Evaluation Criteria (6 dimensions, 5 points each):**

1. **Template Quality & Documentation** - Are templates complete?
2. **Variety & Coverage** - Do templates cover different use cases?
3. **`style.json` Sophistication** - Is it comprehensive and tested?
4. **Quality System Implementation** - Are rubrics specific and linked?
5. **Repository Organization** - Can someone find things easily?
6. **Practical Application** - Is there evidence of real use?

**Scoring:**
- Total: 30 points
- Passing: 18 points (60%)
- Excellent: 26-30 | Good: 22-25 | Adequate: 18-21

### Concept 3: Task Priority Matrix

**Definition:**
An analysis tool that identifies your highest-value AI automation opportunities.

**The Matrix:**
Rate tasks on two dimensions:
- **Frequency (1-5):** How often do you do this?
- **Time Impact (1-5):** How long does it take?
- **Score = Frequency x Time**

**Four Quadrants:**

| Quadrant | Frequency | Time | Priority | Action |
|----------|-----------|------|----------|--------|
| 1 | High | High | Highest | Automate first |
| 2 | High | Low | High | Quick wins |
| 3 | Low | High | Medium | Worth automating |
| 4 | Low | Low | Low | Optional |

**Strategic Value:**
- Identifies where AI adds most value
- Shows which templates to prioritize
- Identifies Block 2 candidates (complex workflows)

### Concept 4: `style.json` Completion

**Definition:**
Your finalized personal voice configuration that ensures consistent AI outputs.

**Complete Structure:**
```json
{
  "metadata": {
    "version": "1.0",
    "last_updated": "YYYY-MM-DD",
    "author": "Your Name"
  },
  "voice": {
    "tone": "professional but approachable",
    "formality": "business casual",
    "personality_traits": ["helpful", "direct", "thoughtful"]
  },
  "writing": {
    "sentence_length": "medium (15-25 words)",
    "paragraph_length": "3-5 sentences",
    "structure": "clear headers, bulleted lists"
  },
  "preferences": {
    "always_use": ["active voice", "concrete examples"],
    "always_avoid": ["jargon", "passive voice"]
  },
  "formatting": {
    "headers": "sentence case",
    "lists": "parallel structure",
    "emphasis": "bold for key terms"
  },
  "domain_specific": {
    "your_field": {
      "terminology": "preferred terms",
      "conventions": "field rules"
    }
  }
}
```

**Testing Requirement:**
Use `style.json` in at least 5 prompts and verify AI follows the preferences.
