## Self-Evaluation Process

### Step 1: Review Your Repository (10 minutes)

Go through your repository and verify:
- [ ] README is comprehensive and accurate
- [ ] All templates are documented
- [ ] Examples contain real AI responses
- [ ] style.json is complete and valid
- [ ] Quality rubrics have specific criteria
- [ ] Task matrix is populated
- [ ] Index is accurate
- [ ] All links work

### Step 2: Score Each Criterion (15 minutes)

Using the rubric below, honestly assess your work on each dimension.

### Step 3: Calculate Total (5 minutes)

Add up your scores and determine your level.

### Step 4: Complete Reflection (5 minutes)

Answer the reflection questions at the end of this guide.
