## Participant Instructions

### What to Do This Week

1. **Complete Your Repository** (if any gaps remain)
   - Ensure all 5+ templates are fully documented
   - Verify style.json is complete and valid
   - Confirm quality rubrics have specific criteria
   - Check task priority matrix is populated

2. **Create Capstone Summary**
   - Document what's in your repository
   - List templates, rubrics, and other components
   - Note key achievements and lessons learned

3. **Complete Self-Evaluation**
   - Use the rubric in the participant guide
   - Score each of the 6 criteria honestly
   - Calculate your total score

4. **Submit Materials**
   - Ensure repository is accessible
   - Submit link and summary by deadline
